    Yunye He (贺　云野)

    I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Laboratory of Complex Trait Genomics, The University of Tokyo.

    My research focuses on genetic analyses of complex traits and diseases. Specifically, I study the genetic architecture of gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer disease, and cerebrovascular diseases such as stroke.

    Research keywords: Complex trait genomics, Statistical Genetics, GWAS, Gastrointestinal diseases, Cerebrovascular diseases, Proteomics
